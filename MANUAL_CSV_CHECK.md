# How to Download CSV from Gmail Manually

Since IMAP is having network/firewall issues, here's how to manually check and download the CSV:

## Step 1: Open Gmail
Go to https://mail.google.com

## Step 2: Look for Emails with CSV Attachments

1. In the search bar, search for: `has:attachment filename:csv`
2. Or search for: `filename:.csv`

This will show all emails with CSV attachments.

## Step 3: Open the Email with CSV

1. Click on the email
2. Look for the attachment at the bottom
3. Click the **Download** button (⬇️) on the CSV file

## Step 4: Save to Your Computer

The CSV will download to your Downloads folder by default.

## Step 5: Move to Project Folder

Move the downloaded CSV to:
